---
title: Theory and International Order
slug: theory-order
---

China's approach to international order should be studied through its recurring legal vocabulary: sovereign equality, development, multilateralism, non-interference, reform of global governance, and opposition to hegemonism.

This guide treats official documents and practice as evidence of an evolving position rather than a fixed doctrine. It asks when China defends existing institutions, when it presses for reform, and when it advances new concepts such as a community of shared future.
